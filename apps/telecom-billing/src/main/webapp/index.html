⚡ Platform

Everything You Need

A complete telecom billing infrastructure built for modern operators.

📊
Real-time CDR Processing
Automated parsing and rating of Call Detail Records with support for voice, data, and SMS services including on-net and cross-net pricing.
🧾
Automated Billing Engine
Monthly billing cycles with automatic invoice generation, tax calculation (14% VAT), and PDF invoice delivery to customers.
👥
Customer Management
Full CRM panel for managing subscribers, contracts, MSISDNs, and rate plan assignments with real-time status tracking.
📦
Service Bundles
Flexible service packages with configurable quotas for voice, data, and SMS. Priority-based consumption ensures free units are used first.
🔒
Credit Management
Per-contract credit limits with automatic blocking when credit is exhausted. Full billing cycle reset at the start of each period.
🐳
Docker Deployment
Fully containerized with Docker Compose. Includes PostgreSQL, Tomcat apps, and scheduled billing cron job for zero-downtime operations.
